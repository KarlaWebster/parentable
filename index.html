Who do we love to work with?

No diagnosis of your child required for services - we focus on skills, not diagnoses.
  • Families that have children with behavioral or educational challenges
  • School aged children or children receiving Early Intervention services or EI aged
  • Children with developmental delays or learning delays
  • Typically developing children that are having difficulty with routines, following directions, or have increased levels of interfering behaviors
  • Typically developing children that are having difficulty with routines, following directions, or have increased levels of interfering behaviors

 

 

How can we help?

What types of skills and supports you can expect from our programming.
  • Parent training services
  • Speech and Language services
  • Counseling services
  • Tutoring services focusing on time on task, attending, and increasing fluency in HW skills
  • Daily living skills such as chores, routines, bedtime problems, meal time behavior and feeding strategies
  • Increasing early learning skills, social skills, and language
  • Learning behavioral strategies to increase positive skills and decrease challenging and interfering behaviors such as aggression and tantrums
